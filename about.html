Our Company

Founded in 2018 by Bafokeng Khoali, Baffy's Computer Solutions has grown to become a trusted provider of comprehensive IT services in Maseru, Lesotho. We specialize in delivering tailored technology solutions to businesses and individuals.

Our Vision

To be the leading provider of innovative and reliable IT solutions in Lesotho, empowering businesses and individuals through technology. We aim to bridge the digital divide by making advanced technology accessible and understandable to all our clients.

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional IT services that drive business growth and efficiency. We are committed to providing customized solutions, outstanding customer service, and continuous innovation to meet the evolving technological needs of our clients.

Bafokeng Khoali

Aspiring Developer | Tech Enthusiast

From a young age, I found myself fixing computers for family and friends—long before I even realized I was stepping into the shoes of an IT technician. That early curiosity grew into a deep love for technology, and today I hold a Diploma in ICT and a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology.

I'm passionate about development and databases, always exploring new tools and techniques to bring ideas to life. I thrive on learning—especially through freelancing, where real-world problems have sharpened my skills and expanded my creativity.

I'm driven by innovation and excited about solving challenges through technology. Whether I'm building a website, designing a database, or helping someone get their system back up and running, I bring energy, commitment, and a desire to keep growing in this fast-paced industry.
