About Baffy's Computer Solutions

Your trusted technology partner in Lesotho

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Baffy's Computer Solutions began as a small IT service provider with a vision to deliver exceptional technology solutions to businesses in Maseru. What started as a one-person operation has grown into a respected IT firm serving clients across Lesotho.

Our journey has been marked by continuous learning, adaptation to new technologies, and an unwavering commitment to client satisfaction. We take pride in having helped over 50 businesses transform their operations through technology.

2018
Company founded with focus on computer repairs and IT support
2022
Expanded into web development and network solutions
2024
Launched mobile and web app development services
